1) Origin and context of Be Mad, Behead

1.1) Who is creating it, and who takes care of what?

This projet is the creation of 42gats and Talk-n-draw alias Hugo and Léa (you can find us on Instagram and Twitter). Hugo is the programmer, I (Léa) take care of the game art and we both work on the story, the gameplay and the game design.

1.2) What gave us this idea and when will it be released?

This game was Hugo's idea, but was developped by the both of us. We'll be working on it for the next few years while we go to college for the next 5 years.

3) Details of the Game (spoilers warning)

3.1) Beheading Gameplay

Every time you kill a Boss, you'll have to behead it and add it to the Heads you already have. The slots available for the heads you carry are limited, so be sure to collect Flesh in pauper's graves in the villages you'll encounter throughout your gameplay.
